About the Theory

Our souls are not bound to the 3D world of space and linear time. They exist in 4D — the realm of spacetime, where the full pattern of our lives is laid out like a tapestry. This is Heaven: a place outside of time, where the soul exists eternally.

But in this same 4D field, evil also moves — not as a horned figure, but as a force that twists the flow of time, memory, and spirit. The devil is a 4D being, influencing past, present, and future.

Only God, who exists beyond time itself, can intervene and realign the soul. The Holy Spirit is the bridge — God's presence within the soul — connecting the timeless to our lived experience.

The Dimensional Hierarchy

  • 3D: The physical world — space, bodies, and linear time.
  • 4D: The soul and Heaven — timeless existence across spacetime.
  • 4D (Devil): A force of corruption acting across time.
  • 5D+: God — beyond time, source of creation and salvation.

Spiritual Implications

This theory explains why life, death, and spiritual warfare are not just metaphorical, but dimensional realities. It reframes prayer, faith, and salvation as interactions with higher-dimensional forces that transcend time.

Death is simply the 3D body losing contact with the 4D soul, and Heaven is where the soul eternally resides — timeless and whole.
